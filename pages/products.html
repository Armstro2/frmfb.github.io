Our Products

Comprehensive financial solutions tailored to your needs — from savings to loans, we have you covered.

What We Offer

Banking Products for Every Need

Royal Savings Deposit Account

Competitive interest rates. Save daily, weekly, quarterly, or yearly. SMS alerts included. Can be used as loan collateral. Opening deposit: N1,000.

Joint Savings Account

Save together with a partner. Perfect for couples, business partners, and families who want to build wealth collectively.

Fixed Term Deposit

Lock in your savings for a fixed period and earn higher interest rates. Flexible tenures available to match your goals.

Save for Christmas

A special savings plan to help you prepare for the festive season. Start early, celebrate better with your loved ones.

Super Kid

A savings account designed for children. Teach your kids the value of saving from an early age and secure their future.

Royal Current Account

Business transactions and salary withdrawals. Customized cheque book, monthly statements, overdraft access. Opening: N5,000.

Assets Acquisition Scheme

Finance the purchase of assets and equipment for your business with flexible repayment terms and competitive rates.

Corporate Current Account

Tailored for corporate entities. Multi-user access, bulk transactions, and dedicated relationship management.

Co-operative Account

Specialized accounts for co-operative societies with group management features and bulk transaction capabilities.

Esusu (ABC) Thrift Scheme

Traditional thrift collection system modernized. Regular contributions, rotating disbursements — a trusted community savings model.

Royal Bliz Account

A streamlined current account with simplified requirements for quick onboarding and immediate banking access.

Fixed Term Deposit

Current account holders can also enjoy fixed deposit benefits with competitive returns and flexible tenure options.

Microcredit Scheme

Loans for market traders, co-operatives, and groups at minimal interest rates. Build savings while you borrow and grow.

Co-operative Loans

Tailored financing for registered co-operative societies and associations with group guarantee structures.

ABC Loan

Our Ajo-Business-Cooperative loan product for individual entrepreneurs and small business owners seeking capital.

ABC Group Loan

Group-based lending with collective guarantee. Ideal for market associations and trade groups seeking joint financing.

Royal Bliz Loan

Quick access loan product with streamlined application and fast disbursement for urgent financial needs.

Civil Servant Loan

Specialized loans for government employees with convenient salary deduction repayment plans and competitive rates.

Salary Loan

Access loans based on your salary income. Competitive rates with flexible repayment periods up to 24 months.

Assets Loan

Finance the purchase of vehicles, equipment, and other assets with our assets acquisition loan product.

Get Started

How to Open an Account

Opening an account with First Royal MFB is simple and straightforward.

Step 1: Gather Documents

Valid ID (National ID, Voter's Card, Driver's License, or Passport), utility bill, and 2 passport photographs.

Step 2: Visit a Branch

Come to our head office at 12 Chalmer Street, Calabar, or any of our agent locations with your documents.

Step 3: Start Banking

Make your opening deposit and start enjoying our full range of banking services immediately.
