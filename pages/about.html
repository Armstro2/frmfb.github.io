Our Story

From Community Bank to Microfinance Leader

First Royal Microfinance Bank Limited was formerly known as Mbukpa Area Community Bank Limited, incorporated on 25th May, 2004 to carry on community bank business in Nigeria.

Following the Central Bank of Nigeria's policy on microfinance banking, we converted to First Royal Microfinance Bank Limited on 17th September, 2007, receiving Microfinance Bank license No: 000026 granted by the Central Bank of Nigeria.

Since then, we have grown in leaps and bounds, expanding our product offerings and embracing digital innovation while staying true to our mission of financial inclusion. Our corporate headquarters is located at 12, Chalmer Street, Calabar, Cross River State, Nigeria.
