Nationwide Access

Banking Outside Calabar

It is now possible to deposit and withdraw from your FRMFB account anywhere in Nigeria through our partnership with Sterling Bank Plc.

01

Deposit Anywhere

Deposit into our FRMFB correspondent account at any Sterling Bank Plc branch nationwide. Just write your account name and number on the deposit slip.

02

Withdraw Anywhere

Use your FRMFB co-branded ATM card at any Sterling Bank ATM or branch to withdraw cash from your account.

03

Use Cheques

Issue your FRMFB co-branded cheques with Sterling Bank Plc to third parties for clearing — takes just 3 working days!
