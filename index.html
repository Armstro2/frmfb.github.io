About Us

Building Prosperity Together Since 2005

First Royal Microfinance Bank Limited is a CBN-licensed financial institution dedicated to providing inclusive banking services to individuals, micro-entrepreneurs, and small businesses across Nigeria.

With a deep understanding of the Nigerian financial landscape, we have designed our products and services to meet the unique needs of our diverse clientele. Our commitment to financial inclusion drives everything we do.

We believe everyone deserves access to quality financial services, regardless of income level or background. That's why we've built a bank that truly puts people first.
