About Us

Two Decades of Financial Excellence

First Royal Microfinance Bank Limited was incorporated on 25th May, 2004 as Mbukpa Area Community Bank Limited. Converted to a microfinance bank on 17th September, 2007, we have grown in leaps and bounds, delivering accessible financial services to individuals, businesses, and communities across Nigeria.

Our Purpose

What Drives Us

Our Mission

To create wealth for the active poor by providing quality, professional and efficient financial services and resources in an ethical and economically sustainable manner.

Our Vision

To be the leading customer responsive and environmentally friendly microfinance bank, delivering affordable, sustainable world class microfinance services.

Our Values

Commitment, Honesty, Integrity, Love, Discipline, and Quality — the pillars that guide every decision we make in serving our customers and communities.
