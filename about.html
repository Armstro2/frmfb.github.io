Our Story

Who We Are

Your Trusted Partner in Financial Growth

First Royal Microfinance Bank Limited was established in 2005 with a singular vision: to bridge the financial inclusion gap in Nigeria by providing accessible, affordable, and reliable banking services to individuals and micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs) who were underserved by traditional banks.

Over the past 18+ years, we have grown from a single branch operation to a network of 12 branches across multiple states, serving over 19,000 active customers. Our success is built on a deep understanding of our customers' needs and an unwavering commitment to their financial well-being.

We are licensed and regulated by the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN), and all customer deposits are fully insured by the Nigeria Deposit Insurance Corporation (NDIC), giving you complete peace of mind.
