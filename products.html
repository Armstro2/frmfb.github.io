Our flagship savings account is designed to help you build a secure financial future. With competitive interest rates calculated daily and paid monthly, your money works as hard as you do.

  • Opening balance of just ₦1,000
  • Competitive interest rate of up to 6% per annum
  • Free debit card for easy access
  • Mobile and internet banking included
  • Free SMS and email alerts
  • Standing order instructions available

Key Features:

Fixed Deposit

Fixed Deposit Account

Maximize your returns with our Fixed Deposit account. Lock in your funds for a fixed tenor and enjoy premium interest rates that grow your wealth predictably.

Key Features:

  • Minimum deposit: ₦50,000
  • Tenor options: 30, 60, 90, 180, or 365 days
  • Attractive interest rates up to 12% per annum
  • Interest can be paid upfront or at maturity
  • Automatic rollover option available
  • Can be used as collateral for loans
